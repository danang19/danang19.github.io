DIGITAL LANGUAGE SYSTEM FEATURES

1) Multimedia Learning with Movie Teleco enables to

a. create and distribute multimedia materials easily. 

b. have students practice in the class as well as by self-learning.

2) Virtual Tutorial functions enables to

a. monitor students’ activity, conversation and screen. 

b. have one-on-one conversation with a student.

c. share a student’s screen/speech within the class as a model presenter.

d. conduct pair/group conversations of the students.

e. assign groupwork and promote collaboration within the group.

3) Class Management functions enables to

a. take attendance and summarize the attendance data. 

b. distribute, collect and redistribute any files to the class.

c. have students submit file to the teacher.

d. start application programs on the students’ desktop.

e. restrict the use of PC and Internet at any time.

4) Assessment functions enables teachers to

a. conduct surveys and summarize the result with Response Analyzer. 

b. create, distribute, collect and mark Quizzes and summarize the result.

DIGITAL LANGUAGE SYSTEM FEATURES

Course Management

Instructors can create course pages for each course and add learning modules to each lesson. Learners can log in at anytime and from anywhere to access their course materials, files, discussion and assignments 24/7. 

File Manager 

 All resources for each course and lesson files can be centrally managed. 

Calendar 

Users can add their schedules and tasks to manage. 

e-Portfolio 

Learners can assemble their work, lesson materials and their own recordings for self or 

third person evaluation or for presentation purposes. Instructors or third parties can have the ability toevaluate each learner‘s portfolio.

Announcement 

Administrators can post announcements to all users. 

User Role Management 

The roles of CaLabo Bridge are to facilitate controlling what a user can and cannot do within a course. There are 7 predefined roles. 

CaLabo EX / LX Integration 

Once both CaLabo Bridge and CaLabo EX / LX are linked, they will be able to share all course and enrolled student information.
